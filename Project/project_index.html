My desire is to help people and build meaningful relationships no matter where my journey takes me.

My Story

I was born and raised in Portland, Oregon. My family is originally from the Chicago area, and that has influenced my life a suprising amount (who in their right mind would choose thin-crust pizza?). Being from Portland has instilled an appreciation of the natural beauty that surrounds us and the importance of granting people access to enjoy it, as well as protecting it as best we can. As I said initially, I love exploring the outdoors; hiking, backpacking, traveling, and exploring are among my favorite things to do. As an interesting dichotomy, I'm also fascinated by the cutting edge of the robotics field, especially in the self/teleoperated driving sector and human-labor/human-mimicking sector. There is so much possible innovation to make the human experience richer and to make resources and technology/learning more accessible. I'm also interested in understanding the cost to those who would be displaced due to automation of their career.

I hope that this website gives you some insight into who I am and what I'm up to. If you want to reach out to me, just use the "Contact Me" page. Thanks!
